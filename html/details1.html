Research Paper Presentation

Description :
Research Revelation is a platform at Tech Samaaroh V4.0 where participants will present their research papers, unveiling novel insights and findings. Each presenter will showcase their research journey, methodologies, and outcomes, contributing to the collective pool of knowledge and innovation.
Rounds :
Each presenter will have the opportunity to present their research paper to the audience and panel of judges.
Following the presentation, there will be a Q&A session where participants can delve deeper into their research and address inquiries from the audience and judges.
