AImagination


Description :

Dive into the future of design with AI-magination! This Tech Samaaroh V4.0 competition challenges you to push creative boundaries using cutting-edge AI tools. Get ready to "Design Like Never Before" and bring your artistic vision to life!

Rules and Guidelines :

• It will be a solo event.
• Participants must bring their own devices with the necessary software.
• Participants must adhere to time limits in each round: 8 minutes for the 1st & 2nd round and 15 minutes for the 3rd round.
• Any form of plagiarism or unethical behavior will result in disqualification.
• Topics for the 1st & 2nd rounds will be provided by the organizer at the time of the event.

Rounds :

1. Round 1 (Draw-AI-Thon)
In the first round, participants draw random topics from a bowl and must swiftly generate images based on their chosen subjects.
Time Limit: 8 Minutes
2. Round 2 (Surprise AI Remix)
In the second round, participants receive an image to work with. Using any AI tools they prefer, they must modify and tweak the image based on a surprise task given during the round. It's a test of their creativity and adaptability with AI.
Time Limit: 8 Minutes
3. Round 3 (Pixel Wars)
For the third round, participants are tasked with creating product-based posters. They must blend AI-generated elements with their design skills and personal creativity using software such as Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, etc. The challenge lies in seamlessly combining artificial intelligence with individual artistic flair to craft compelling posters.
Time Limit: 15 Minutes
