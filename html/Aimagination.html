AImagination


Description :
AI-magination is a design competition at Tech Samaaroh V4.0 that challenges participants to push the boundaries of creativity using AI tools. Over three rounds, participants will create designs based on a given topic using artificial intelligence and then manipulate them to achieve their artistic vision.
Rounds :
There will be three rounds each round will involve using AI to generate design elements and then manipulating them creatively.
