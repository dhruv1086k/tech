Description:
The Tecky Recky Relay Race is an innovative team competition that merges design thinking with the thrill of a relay race. Teams of four will compete, alternating between bursts of speed and tackling design challenges strategically placed at each turn. These challenges will test the participants problem-solving skills, creativity, and ability to think under pressure.
