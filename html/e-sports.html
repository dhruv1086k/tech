E-sports competition at Tech Samaaroh V4.0 emphasizes the skills and passion towards gaming through participating in various games and having a man on battle matches with their rivals. Rounds:
BGMI :
A single custom room of 100 players battle royale (ERANGLE) will be played. 5 players are allowed to register (1 substitute and 4 players).
VALORANT :

Matches will be played in knockout style.
Mk11 :
Matches will be played in 1v1 brackets (brackets will be allotted to players beforehand). Matches will be played in knockout style.
