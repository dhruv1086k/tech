Event Details

Tech-Samaaroh is an annual extravaganza hosted by the Trinity Institute of Professional Studies, Dwarka, designed to celebrate the spirit of technology, innovation, and creativity. It's a platform where students from all walks of life come together to showcase their talents, learn from industry experts, and immerse themselves in the ever-evolving world of technology.
Prepare yourself for a series of exhilarating events that will challenge your skills, ignite your passion, and leave you craving more! Here's a glimpse of what awaits you:

AI-imagination : Push the boundaries of creativity using AI tools and unleash your artistic vision.
Tecky-Recky : Merge design thinking with the thrill of a relay race and test your problem-solving skills under pressure.
Gaming E-sports : Showcase your gaming skills in a battle of champions featuring games like MK-11, BGMI, and Valorant.
Flag Pursuit : Navigate through cyber security challenges, uncovering hidden codes and messages while sharpening your programming skills.
Research Paper Presentation : Unveil novel insights and findings through your research papers, contributing to the collective pool of knowledge.
Ideathon : Present your groundbreaking ideas aimed at addressing real-world challenges and making a positive impact.
Web Dev Showdown : Put your coding skills to the test and build innovative and user-friendly websites in a limited time.
Ideathon : Present your innovative ideas and turn them into real-world tech solutions in a thrilling build phase.
Wordcraft : Craft compelling compositions showcasing your proficiency in written communication.
MindFest : Test your knowledge across various topics in an exciting trivia challenge.

So, mark your calendars and get ready to embark on a journey of discovery, innovation, and excitement at Tech-Samaaroh 4.0! We can't wait to see you there!
